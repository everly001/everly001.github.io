About Me

Hello, my name is Everly. I'm a Python developer based in London, UK. My interest in web development started back in 2017 when I was working with eCommerce clients to develop websites on the Shopify platform, though my role was strategy consulting, I found myself learning HTML, CSS & Javascript to fix various problems that arose with their themes. From there I got more and more involved with the web development side of things as I really enjoyed designing and developing better user experiences than what the original templates allowed.

In January of 2020 just before the pandemic hit I made the decision to go all in on learning to code as my interest in tech had grown more and more during my time developing eCommerce websites, I started researching about programming and the various languages and made the choice to start learning Python. Since then I have completed hundreds of hours of study from the foundations of Python, to Django to databases to data structures and algorithms, and built some projects of my own!
